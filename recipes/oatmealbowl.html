Oatmeal Bowl

Description

This is a delicious recipe that combines flavors to create a mouthwatering dish. It's perfect for a protein packed breakfast or a cozy night in.

Serves: 1

Calories: 680 Kcal Of which Protein: 60 g

Ingredients

  • 80 g rolled oats- 300 Kcal
  • 31 g scoop of vanilla whey protein- 120 Kcal
  • 170 g serving of vanilla nonfat non sugar yogurt- 70 kcal
  • 16 g Dark Chocolate chips- 80 kcal
  • 240 ml fairlife milk nonfat- 80 kcal
  • 5 g Ghirardelli Cocoa powder unsweetened- 20 Kcal

Steps

  1. Step 1: To an empty bowl, add 80 g rolled oats.
  2. Step 2: Add the scoop of whey protein and mix the dry whey in the oats to distribute it.
  3. Step 3: Add the cocoa powder and chocolate chips to the dry mix.
  4. Step 4: Add the yogurt and milk to the dry mix and stir until all the ingredients are combined.
